🌡️ Heat and Cool Materials Simulator

An interactive educational web application that helps students understand how different materials respond to heating and cooling. The simulator allows users to predict which materials heat up or cool down the fastest and provides explanations based on thermal conductivity and specific heat capacity.

📚 Project Overview

The Heat and Cool Materials Simulator is designed for school students to learn the concepts of:
.Heating and cooling of materials
.Thermal conductivity
.Specific heat capacity
.Temperature change behaviour of common substances
Students can interact with different materials, make predictions, and immediately receive feedback along with scores.

🎯 Learning Objectives

After using this simulator, students will be able to:
.Compare different materials based on their thermal properties.
.Understand why some materials heat faster than others.
.Learn how specific heat capacity affects temperature change.
.Develop prediction and observation skills.

🔬 Scientific Principle Used

The simulator estimates temperature change using:
Rate = Thermal Conductivity ÷ Specific Heat Capacity
Materials with:
1.Higher conductivity
2.Lower specific heat tend to heat and cool faster.
