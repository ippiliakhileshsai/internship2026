The Effects of Heating and Cooling project is an interactive educational simulation developed to help children understand basic scientific concepts in a fun and engaging way. The application allows students to explore how different objects react when heat or cooling is applied through virtual experiments.

The simulation includes activities such as melting chocolate, observing the expansion and contraction of a balloon, and understanding steam formation from hot water. By interacting with these experiments, students can visually learn important concepts like melting, solidification, evaporation, expansion, and contraction.

The project combines education with technology by providing a child-friendly interface, interactive controls, progress tracking, and a reward system. This approach makes learning more enjoyable and encourages students to develop curiosity and scientific thinking skills.
