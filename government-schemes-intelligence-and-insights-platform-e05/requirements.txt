PROJECT OBJECTIVES :

---> Develop a web-based platform that provides information and insights about various Government Schemes in India.
---> Enable users to search, explore, and understand different schemes based on categories and eligibility. 
---> Provide data visualization and analytics for scheme trends and usage.
---> Use a simple and user-friendly interface for easy navigation. 
---> Include filtering and search functionalities for quick access to schemes. 
---> Ensure accurate and up-to-date information from trusted government sources. 
---> Provide insights and recommendations related to government schemes.

FEATURES :

---> Scheme Search and Exploration 
---> User-friendly Dashboard
---> Data Visualization and Analytics 
---> Eligibility Information 
---> Search and Filter Options 
---> Scheme Categories 
---> Reports and Insights 
---> Easy Navigation and Responsive Design
