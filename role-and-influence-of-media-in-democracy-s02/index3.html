PILLAR 01

Watchdog

CORE FUNCTION

Holds public officials and institutions accountable. Exposes corruption, abuse of power, and factual manipulation.

CIVIC INFLUENCE

Exposes corruption and helps citizens hold leaders accountable.

REAL-WORLD EXAMPLE

Journalists reveal misuse of public funds, leading to investigations and audits.
