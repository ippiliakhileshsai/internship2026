## Overview

This project presents a storyboard that highlights the hidden dangers of electric water pumps used in agricultural fields. Through a real-life-inspired scenario, the story follows a young student who discovers the science behind electric current, circuits, resistance, and earthing after a tragic accident in the village.

The storyboard simplifies complex electrical concepts into an engaging and relatable narrative, helping students and community members understand how electricity behaves and how proper safety measures can prevent accidents.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people do not fully understand how electric circuits work or how electricity flows through different materials.

A major misconception is that water itself causes electric shock. In reality, when a person's skin becomes wet, the body's electrical resistance decreases significantly, allowing electric current to pass through more easily. This can turn the human body into a pathway for electricity to reach the ground, leading to severe injuries or even death.

Lack of awareness about electrical safety, damaged wiring, and improper earthing continues to put farmers and rural communities at risk.

---

## Proposed Solution

Our project uses storytelling and visual learning to explain the concepts of Electric Current and Circuits in a simple and memorable way.

### Key Solutions:

* Explain the basics of electric current and electric circuits.
* Demonstrate how electricity flows through a complete circuit.
* Show the effect of wet skin on electrical resistance.
* Illustrate the importance of proper earthing in electrical systems.
* Create awareness about electrical hazards around water pumps and farms.
* Encourage safe practices when handling electrical equipment.

---

## Expected Outcomes

By the end of the storyboard, learners will:

* Understand the fundamentals of electric current and circuits.
* Recognize the dangers of electrical equipment near water.
* Learn why wet conditions increase the risk of electric shock.
* Understand the role of earthing in preventing accidents.
* Develop awareness of electrical safety practices.
* Apply scientific concepts to solve real-world problems.
* Promote safer environments in farms and rural communities.

---

## Project Goal

To transform a real-world electrical safety issue into an engaging educational experience that helps students and communities learn science while saving lives through awareness and prevention.
