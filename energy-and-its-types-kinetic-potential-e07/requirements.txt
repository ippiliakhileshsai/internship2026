Energy Simulation: Kinetic Energy and Potential Energy Visualization

Project Objective

To create an interactive web-based simulation that demonstrates the conversion between Potential Energy (PE) and Kinetic Energy (KE) while showing that Total Mechanical Energy remains conserved.

Software Requirements:

Front-End Technologies
HTML5
CSS3
JavaScript
Libraries Used
chartjs.org⁠  (for energy graph visualization)
Development Tools
Visual Studio Code
Web Browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)

Hardware Requirements:

Minimum Hardware
Processor: Intel i3 or equivalent
RAM: 4 GB
Storage: 500 MB free space
Recommended Hardware
Processor: Intel i5 or above
RAM: 8 GB
Internet connection (for loading Chart.js CDN)

Functional Requirements:

1. Parameter Adjustment
The user should be able to modify:
Height (m)
Mass (kg)
Speed (m/s)
2. Energy Calculation
The system should calculate:
Potential Energy (PE = m × g × h)
Kinetic Energy (KE = ½ × m × v²)
Total Energy (TE = PE + KE)
3. Simulation Animation
Ball movement on the hill.
Visual representation of energy transformation.
4. Graph Visualization
Display energy values using a bar chart.
Update graph dynamically whenever values change.
5. Reset Functionality
Return all parameters and animation to default state.
