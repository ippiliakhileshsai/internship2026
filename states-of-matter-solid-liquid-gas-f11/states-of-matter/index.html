💡 How it works
  • 🌡️ Temperature — Slide up to add heat energy. Particles vibrate faster → melt → boil → plasma!
  • 🌀 Pressure — High pressure squeezes particles closer together. Low pressure (vacuum) lets them spread out freely.
  • 🔬 4 States — Solid (fixed lattice), Liquid (flowing), Gas (free moving), Plasma (ionized). Click any button to switch!
  • 🔍 Substance Search — Type any substance (gold, water, chocolate…) to see its real melting & boiling points.
  • ⚡ Auto Phase Change — Adjust temperature and watch particles automatically change state at the right temperature!
  • 🧠 Quiz yourself — Click the Quiz button to test your knowledge in English, Hindi or Telugu.

🔬 Why This State?

In a solid, particles are tightly packed in a regular lattice structure. The intermolecular forces are strong enough to keep particles in fixed positions — they only vibrate in place. This is why solids have definite shape and volume.
