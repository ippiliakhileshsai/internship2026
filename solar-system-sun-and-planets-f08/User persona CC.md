**User persona**

**About** 

I am a curious 7th-grade student who enjoys learning about space, planets, and science. I like watching educational videos and using interactive apps to understand difficult topics.

**Learning Context**

I learn science mainly through textbooks and classroom teaching. Sometimes I find it difficult to imagine how planets move around the Sun just by looking at pictures in books.

**Goals \& Dreams**

Learn about the Solar System in a fun way.

Understand planets, moons, and space missions.

Improve science knowledge and school performance.

Become a scientist or space researcher in the future.

**Challenges \& Pain Points**

Science concepts can be difficult to visualize.

Textbooks provide limited interaction.

English scientific terms are sometimes hard to understand.

Traditional teaching methods can feel boring.

**Needs \& Expectations**

Interactive 3D models.

Simple explanations.

Fun quizzes.

**Motivations**

Love for space.

Better grades.

Learning through games.

**Technology Comfort**

Uses mobile apps regularly.

Can navigate educational websites.

**Quotes \& Insights**

"Learning about planets becomes exciting when it feels like a game."

Additional Notes

Prefers visual, interactive, and game-based learning.
