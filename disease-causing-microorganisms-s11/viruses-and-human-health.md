# Viruses and Human Health

## Introduction

Viruses are microscopic infectious agents that reproduce only inside living cells. They can infect humans, animals, and plants and cause different diseases.

---

## Common Viral Diseases

## 1. COVID-19

### Cause
COVID-19 is caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

### Symptoms
- Fever
- Cough
- Breathing difficulty
- Loss of taste or smell
- Tiredness

### Prevention
- Vaccination
- Maintain hygiene
- Wash hands regularly
- Avoid contact with infected people


---

## 2. Dengue

### Cause
Dengue is caused by dengue virus spread through infected mosquitoes.

### Symptoms
- High fever
- Severe headache
- Joint and muscle pain
- Skin rash

### Prevention
- Prevent mosquito breeding
- Avoid stagnant water
- Use mosquito nets


---

## 3. Flu (Influenza)

### Cause
Influenza is caused by influenza viruses.

### Symptoms
- Fever
- Cough
- Runny nose
- Body pains

### Prevention
- Take flu vaccines
- Maintain hygiene
- Cover mouth while coughing


---

## 4. AIDS

### Cause
AIDS is caused by Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV).

### Symptoms
- Weak immune system
- Frequent infections
- Weight loss

### Prevention
- Avoid sharing needles
- Follow safe medical practices
- Awareness about HIV transmission


---

# General Prevention of Viral Infections

- Vaccination
- Personal hygiene
- Healthy lifestyle
- Clean surroundings
