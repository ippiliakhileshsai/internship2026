Welcome to the World of Disease-Causing Microorganisms

Microorganisms are tiny living organisms that cannot be seen with the naked eye. Some are useful, while others can cause infectious diseases in humans. This app helps learners explore microbes, related illnesses, and the best prevention habits.

What are microorganisms?

They are very small organisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa that can affect living beings.

How do they cause disease?

Harmful microbes enter the body, multiply, damage tissues, and spread through air, food, water, or contact.

Why should we learn this?

Understanding infection helps people identify symptoms early and protect themselves and their communities.
