Project Title

Earth's 4 Spheres Interactive Learning Platform

Description of the Project

Earth's 4 Spheres Interactive Learning Platform is an interactive educational website designed to help students understand the Atmosphere, Hydrosphere, Geosphere, and Biosphere. The platform uses visual learning, animations, simulations, quizzes, and interactive activities to make Earth Science concepts easy and engaging for children.

 Problem Statement

Many students find it difficult to understand Earth's four spheres and how they interact with each other through traditional textbook-based learning. Static diagrams and lengthy explanations often make scientific concepts difficult to visualize and remember.

 Solution (What the Project Does)

This project provides an interactive learning experience where students can explore each of Earth's four spheres, understand their real-life examples, visualize the water cycle through simulations, and test their knowledge through quizzes. The website encourages learning through interaction, observation, and exploration rather than passive reading.
