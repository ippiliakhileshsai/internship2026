Documentation on Plastic: Types, Properties & Environmental Issues

1. Introduction

Plastics are synthetic polymers widely used due to durability, low cost, and versatility. This documentation covers major plastic types, properties, and environmental impacts.

2. Objectives

  1. Identify common plastic types and resin codes
  2. Explain physical + chemical properties
  3. Analyze environmental issues
  4. Suggest mitigation measures

3. Classification of Plastics

Resin CodePlastic TypeKey PropertiesApplications
1PETLightweight, transparentBottles, food containers
2HDPEHigh strength, chemical resistantMilk jugs, pipes
3PVCRigid, durablePipes, cables, flooring
4LDPEFlexible, moisture resistantPlastic bags, wraps
5PPHeat resistant, toughContainers, lab equipment
6PSRigid, insulatingCups, packaging foam
7OtherMixed propertiesPolycarbonate, nylon

4. Environmental Issues

  1. Non-biodegradability: 100-1000 years to decompose
  2. Marine Pollution: 11M tons enter oceans annually
  3. Carbon Footprint: ~400M tons CO2/year from production
  4. Low Recycling Rate: Only ~9% recycled globally
  5. Toxic Leaching: BPA, phthalates contaminate water/soil

5. Mitigation Measures

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Bioplastics like PLA/PHA, Extended Producer Responsibility policies

6. Conclusion

Responsible use + better segregation needed. Combined effort from industry, govt, and consumers required.
