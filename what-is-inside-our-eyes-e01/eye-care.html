Eye Care Hub

Discover practical tips, habits, and advice to nurture and protect your amazing vision.

Eat Healthy Food

Carrots, spinach, and fish have special vitamins (like Vitamin A and Omega-3s) that keep your retina functioning well and protect your visual acuity.

Read in Good Lighting

Always make sure the room is bright when reading or looking at screens so your ciliary muscles don't have to strain, avoiding eye fatigue.

Avoid Rubbing

Rubbing your eyes can scratch the cornea or introduce bacteria. If your eyes itch, try blinking rapidly or splashing them gently with cold water instead.

Visit the Eye Doctor

Regular vision checkups are essential! Optometrists have advanced diagnostic tools to inspect your eye's layers, lens focus, and optical health.
