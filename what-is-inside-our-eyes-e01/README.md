👁️ Our Eye – Interactive Human Eye Learning Platform

An interactive educational web application that helps students understand human eye anatomy, vision science, eye health, and brain visual processing through engaging simulations, animations, quizzes, and hands-on activities.

📖 Overview

Our Eye is a modern, responsive, single-page educational platform designed to make learning about the human eye fun and interactive. The application combines visual simulations, interactive diagrams, and educational content to explain how vision works—from light entering the eye to image processing in the brain.
✨ Features

🏠 Home Page
* Introduction to eye anatomy and vision
* Quick access to all learning modules

👁️ Eye Anatomy Module
* Learn about:
    * Cornea
    * Iris
    * Pupil
    * Lens
    * Retina
    * Optic Nerve
      
💡 Light & Vision Simulation
  * Step-by-step journey of light through the eye
  * Real-time focus simulation

👓 Vision Defects Simulator
Understand common vision conditions:
* Normal Vision
* Myopia (Nearsightedness)
* Hyperopia (Farsightedness)
* Astigmatism

🎯 Interactive Activities
*Pupil Simulator
*Lens Focus Lab

🧠 Brain Vision Module

*Learn how the brain processes visual information

📝 Eye Quiz
* Knowledge assessment
* Interactive questions
* Instant feedback
  
❤️ Eye Care & Health

*Educational guidance for eye health
