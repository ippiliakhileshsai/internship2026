First Law Section

Purpose:
Demonstrates inertia.

What to look for:
Stationary object
Apply Force button
Reset button

Interaction:
Click Start
Apply force
Observe motion
Remove force
Observe continued motion or friction effects

Learning outcome:
Understand why objects resist changes in motion.

Second Law Section

Purpose: 
Explore force vs acceleration.

Controls:
Mass slider
Force slider
Start Simulation

Workflow:
Set object mass
Increase applied force
Run simulation
Observe acceleration changes

Expected behavior:
Higher force → greater acceleration
Higher mass → lower acceleration

Third Law Section

Purpose:
Demonstrate action-reaction force pairs.

Possible examples:
Rocket launch
Balloon propulsion
Two carts pushing apart

Workflow:
Trigger interaction
Observe equal and opposite motions
Compare forces visually

Learning outcome:

Forces always occur in pairs.
