Law of Inertia

Things don't move by themselves — they need a push or pull!

⚽
Ball at rest
→
🦵
Kick it!
→
💨⚽
Ball moves!
📌 "No force = No movement!"

Force = Mass × Acceleration

Push harder → go faster! Heavier objects need more force to move.

🛒
Empty cart
vs
🛒📦
Full cart
→
😅
Harder to push!
📌 F = Mass × Acceleration
3

Action & Reaction

Every push has an equal push back! Forces always come in pairs.

🚀
Rocket up
↕
🔥
Gas down
→
🌟
Blast off!
📌 "Every action = opposite reaction!"
