⚡ Learn Conductors & Insulators

⚡ What are Conductors?

Conductors are materials that allow electricity to flow through them easily.


Examples:
  • Copper
  • Silver
  • Aluminum
  • Iron

🛡️ What are Insulators?

Insulators are materials that prevent electricity from flowing easily.


Examples:
  • Rubber
  • Plastic
  • Glass
  • Wood

🔍 Key Differences

Conductors Insulators
Copper Rubber
Silver Plastic
Aluminum Glass

🌍 Real-Life Examples

🏠 House Wiring → Copper

🔌 Charger Cable → Copper + Plastic

🧤 Electrician Gloves → Rubber

⚡ Power Lines → Aluminum
