Welcome Explorer! 🌍

Visualizing the Science 🔍

Toggle the Hidden Science Layers underneath the canvas map to see:

  • Wind Vector Fields: Arrow length and motion represent air speed.
  • Pressure Zones: Warm/Red = High Pressure, Cold/Blue = Low Pressure.
  • AQI Maps: Color grids show localized smoke particle concentrations.
